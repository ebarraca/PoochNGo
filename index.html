SOME OUR HAPPY CLIENTS

SERVICES

DOG WALKING

Dogs need at least 30 minutes of exercise each day. Let us walk and play with your dog to help them live to their fullest when life gets too busy for you. Our professional, loving dog walkers will come to your home, leash up your furry friend and take them on a walk during a 30, 45 or 60-minute visit.

PET SITTING

While you're away let us visit your dog, cat or small pet to make sure they have all the food, water and company they need. With our 30, 45 or 60-minute pet visits your pet will receive the care, love and attention they crave and deserve! Overnight stays also available if needed.

BOARDING

Boarding is the perfect solution for when you travel and want your dog to stay in a home away from home that is safe, reliable and fun. Our staff go the extra mile to provide the best care possible, in a clean and safe environment that your dog will love.

HOW LONG IS A DOG WALK?

A standard visit will last about 45 minutes. Usually we will walk your dog for 20-30 minutes, depending on your dogs needs. We stick around and play with your dog for a bit if that is welcomed as well. Visits//walks can be longer or shorter as needed as well. Price will vary.

WHAT IF MY PET REQUIRES MEDICATION?

No worries. We can discuss ahead of time and make sure a complete understanding is met regarding your pet's medicine.
