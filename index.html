Yes, Pooch N' Go always has a sit down meeting before anything so you can get to meet the caretaker of your furry friend and ask any questions you may have.

CAN I SCHEUDLE SERVICES LAST MINUTE?

Pooch N' Go understands things can be unexpected and can try to be available on short notice and accomodate your needs. Price may vary depending on how soon the sitting is needed.

HOW LONG IS A DOG WALK?

A standard visit will last about 45 minutes. Usually we will walk your dog for 20-30 minutes, depending on your dogs needs. We stick around and play with your dog for a bit if that is welcomed as well. Visits//walks can be longer or shorter as needed as well. Price will vary.

WHAT IF MY PET REQUIRES MEDICATION?

No worries. We can discuss ahead of time and make sure a complete understanding is met regarding your pet's medicine.
